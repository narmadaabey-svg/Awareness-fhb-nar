What just happened
You scanned a QR code claiming your ERHMIS credentials were at risk and needed updating — without verifying where it came from.
A real attacker could have used a fake login page to steal your ERHMIS username and password, giving them full access to sensitive health data.
QR codes hide their destination URL — you have no way of knowing where you're going until it's too late.
What to do next time
Never update your ERHMIS credentials via a QR code, link, or email — always log in directly through the official system URL.
Legitimate IT teams will never ask for your password via a poster, email, or QR code.
If you receive a suspicious alert about your account, call IT directly to verify before taking any action.

Results from this exercise will be shared with the team anonymously.
The goal is awareness, not blame. Well done for now knowing what to look for.
